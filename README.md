# 📩 AI Customer Support Email Triage System using n8n

An AI-powered customer support automation workflow built with **n8n**, **Google Gemini AI**, **Gmail**, **Google Sheets**, and **Telegram**. This project automatically monitors incoming support emails, classifies customer inquiries using AI, assigns priorities, detects sentiment, logs tickets into Google Sheets, sends Telegram notifications for urgent issues, and replies to customers automatically.

Developed as part of my **30-Day n8n Automation Portfolio**, this workflow demonstrates how AI can streamline customer support operations through intelligent email triage.

---

# 📌 Features

* 📥 Monitors incoming Gmail messages
* 🤖 Uses Google Gemini AI for email classification
* 🏷️ Categorizes customer support requests
* 🚨 Assigns ticket priority automatically
* 😊 Detects customer sentiment
* 📝 Generates AI-powered email summaries
* 📊 Logs support tickets to Google Sheets
* 📲 Sends Telegram alerts for high-priority tickets
* 📧 Sends automatic acknowledgment emails

# ⚙ Workflow Explanation

## 1. Gmail Trigger

Monitors incoming customer support emails.

**Captured Information**

* Sender
* Subject
* Email Body
* Timestamp

---

## 2. Google Gemini AI

Analyzes incoming emails and extracts structured information.

### AI Output

```json
{
  "category": "Technical",
  "priority": "High",
  "sentiment": "Negative",
  "summary": "Customer cannot log into their account after password reset.",
  "reply": "Thank you for contacting us. We have received your request and our support team is reviewing it."
}
```

---

## 3. Code Node

Parses the JSON response generated by Google Gemini into structured data for workflow processing.

---

## 4. Merge Node

Combines Gmail metadata with the AI-generated output before routing the workflow.

---

## 5. IF Node

Routes tickets based on priority.

**High Priority**

* Send Telegram notification
* Send acknowledgment email
* Log ticket to Google Sheets

**Medium / Low Priority**

* Send acknowledgment email
* Log ticket to Google Sheets

---

## 6. Google Sheets

Stores all customer support tickets.

### Logged Information

| Timestamp | Sender | Subject | Category | Priority | Sentiment | Summary | Status |
| --------- | ------ | ------- | -------- | -------- | --------- | ------- | ------ |

---

## 7. Telegram

Sends instant notifications for high-priority support requests.

Example:

```text
🚨 High Priority Support Ticket

Category: Technical
Priority: High

Summary:
Customer cannot log into their account after password reset.
```

---

## 8. Gmail

Sends an automatic acknowledgment email confirming receipt of the customer's request.
